History of Athens

At the Beginning

Athens was the Greek capital of Rome, which was inhabited for around 5000 years. The people who lived in Athens mostly consisted of Greeks who came from Asia. Initially, however, Athens was completely evacuated after 1827 due to various wars that were occurring at the time. The place initially had 4000 people, which grew to 1.5 million Greeks over time after various years and forms of rebuilding.
